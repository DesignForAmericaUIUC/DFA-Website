YMCA Lifeguards

Spring 2019

Pools across the country and world are closing due to lifeguard shortages. Why? Social expectations and technology are shifting and aquatics programs need help adapting to them. There is a need for a more diverse pool of recruits (pun intended), lower barriers of entry to the workforce, strong work culture, and more long-term career incentives.

Community Partners: YMCA

Team Lead: Emmie Fister

Team Members: Sneha Subramanian, Alastair Merrett, Robbie Plank, Annabel Huber, Lauren Cox


Status: In Progress
